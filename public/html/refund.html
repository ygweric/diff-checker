Refund Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to My Tools Life ("we", "our", "us")!

This Refund Policy applies to purchases made on our website mytools.life (the "Site").

If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

2. Eligibility for Refund

2.1. We offer refunds on products that are damaged or defective upon delivery.

2.2. To be eligible for a refund, you must contact us within 7 days of receiving the product and provide proof of the issue (e.g., photographs).

2.3. We do not offer refunds for change of mind or buyer's remorse.

3. How to Request a Refund

3.1. To request a refund, please contact our customer service team at support@mytools.life with your order details and the reason for your refund request.

3.2. We will review your request and respond within 5 business days.

3.3. If your refund is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment. The time it takes for the refund to process may vary depending on your payment provider.
